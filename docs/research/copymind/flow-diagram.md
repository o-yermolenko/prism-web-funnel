## COPYWRITING FORMULAS USED

### Pain Point Questions
- Pattern: "What [verb] you the most?"
- Examples:
  - "What worries you the most?"
  - "What annoys you the most about people?"

### Self-Assessment Questions
- Pattern: "How [adjective] are you [with topic]?"
- Examples:
  - "How extroverted are you?"
  - "How comfortable are you with taking risks?"

### Frequency Questions
- Pattern: "How [often/easily] do you [behavior]?"
- Examples:
  - "How often do you postpone important decisions?"
  - "How easily can you stay focused when making a decision?"

### Reflective Questions
- Pattern: "What do you typically do when [situation]?"
- Example: "What do you typically do when reflecting on past choices?"

### Binary Qualification
- Pattern: "Would you [action] if you could [benefit]?"
- Example: "Would you be satisfied if you could easily and quickly make the right decisions?"
